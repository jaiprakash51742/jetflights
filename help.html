Warning

A number of people have been contacted by phone, WhatsApp and email by individuals claiming to represent Cheapflights, offering to book them a trip and requesting direct credit card or wire transfer payment. Some were also asked to provide their passport and identity card numbers. These direct emails, WhatsApp messages or calls do not come from Cheapflights, but from imposters who are trying to defraud you, or act with other malicious intent. Do not provide your credit card details, nor wire money to any bank account. If you have provided your credit card number, contact the issuing bank to block your card. If you have provided your passport or identity card number, contact your police station.
